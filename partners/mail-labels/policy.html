Privacy Policy for Mail labels

Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 24, 2025

In this document by "App" we mean Mail labels application.
  1. Collected data:

    App accesses your Gmail™ email service account to read:

    • labels to display them in the app
    • email subject to create local notification about new messages in the label

  2. Access: Collected data can be accessed by user ONLY (showing in the app)
  3. Sharing: The collected data will not be shared with anyone in any way.
  4. Security:

    • App fetches data from Gmail™ email service using official Gmail API
    • Authentication implemented using GoogleSignIn-iOS SDK
    • App does not use any other third-party services
