Mail labels iOS app help

The purpose of the app "Mail labels" is to monitor your Gmail™ labels and notify you about new messages in the label.
The app fills the gap in the Gmail™ app, which does not provide notifications for labels on iOS.

The flow of the app is simple:

  1. Open the app
  2. Sign in with your Gmail account
  3. Choose the labels you want to monitor
  4. Close the app
  5. Receive notifications about new messages in the chosen labels
